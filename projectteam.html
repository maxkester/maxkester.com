Cornell University Autonomous Bicycle Project Team


Cornell University's Autonomous Bicycle Project Team aims to build a self-steering, self-balancing, and self-navigating bycicle. I am currently the Systems subteam lead, which means my team's responsibilities are to synthesize, streamline, and service our bikes' electronic hardware and mechanical operations. Explore our GitHub or Website to learn more.
