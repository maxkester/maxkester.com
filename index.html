About Me

I am currently a student at the Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Cornell University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science.

My taste for engineering first developed when I was living in Abu Dhabi; surrounded by geological, civil, and mechanical marvels. Since then, I knew I wanted to be involved in revolutionary systems and solutions with the power to change how we do things.

In this website you will find projects that helped me develop my creative skills, expand my ideation toolkit, explore exciting problems, or strengthen my engineering principles.
