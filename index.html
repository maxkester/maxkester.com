About Me

It all started when I was living in this beautiful city: Abu Dhabi. Surrounded by marvels of geological, civil, biomedical, and mechanical engineering, I wanted to be involved in synthesizing systems that solve real-world problems.

I am currently a student at the Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Cornell University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in - you guessed it - Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Aerospace.

In this site you will find projects that allowed me to develop my creative skills, explore new disciplines, encounter exciting problems, or strengthen my engineering principles.

Interests

On campus, I am a Teaching Assistant, Orientation Leader, contributor to The First Amendment publication, member of two project teams, and club Ultimate Frisbee player. Turns out these activities describe me pretty well; I aim to be active, exercise my academic knowledge, be a source of inspiration to others, and never avoid difficult conversations. When time permits, I play guitar with my band or head over to the local airport to work on my pilot's license in a Cessna-152.

When I'm craving a little nostalgia, I paint Magic: The Gathering card alterations for players at my local card shop. Previous commissions can be found at my deviantArt Gallery.
