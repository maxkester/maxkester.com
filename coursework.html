Peristaltic Pump

  • 2018
  • Autodesk Fusion 360
  • A photorealstic rendering of a Peristaltic Pump model.

Reuleaux Machine

  • 2018
  • Autodesk Fusion 360
  • A photorealistic rendering of a Reuleaux machine model, which translates a rotational power input to deliver a linear power output on a periodical path.

Leatherman

  • 2018
  • Autodesk Fusion 360
  • A model of my leatherman tool, including a flat blade, tooth saw, phillips-head driver, bottle opener, and serated blade.

Architecture Award

  • 2014
  • Wood, LEGOs, Mechatronics
  • The final project for my second year of Design & Technology class. The goal was to design and build an award that reflected the discipline being commemerated. My product was influenced by the Burj Khalifa in Dubai.

Steampunk Lamp

  • 2013
  • Wood, LEGOs, Mechatronics
  • My first proper experience with designing and creating a product, for GCSE Design & Technology. The brown and silver color scheme, asymetrical lightbulbs, and exposed wiring combined to achieve the industrial atmosphere that is a cornerstone of "steampunk" design.
