Cable Retention

  • 2018
  • SolidWorks 2014, PLA
  • A 3D printed cable bracket meant to be installed on the back of a Zotac computer. Using zipties it secured HDMI, USB, power, etc. cables which minimized data loss and risk of cable detachment.
